The firefighter code

The mentality of a firefighter is very similar to that of a software engineer, surprisingly. Firefighters train repeatedly with precision. When they respond to an emergency seemingly in chaos they are able to act by muscle memory, line by line and step by step, just as they practiced. Each training drill is a function. First you learn to successfully complete the function. Then you practice to reduce the steps needed and time required to accomplish the same task.

Tech and $$$

While working with clients I interview them to better understand their financial goals and needs. Transition

With the onset of COVID-19 and the changing political landscape surrounding the financial world I became uneasy with the volnerability in the longevity as a Financial Advisor. Researching an old passion I knew that Software Engineering would be the right fit for our family. I completed a 4 month, 90+ hour per week, coding camp. We focused on front-end design and full stack development in Python, JAVA and ReactJS. I have been building projects in each language. Great knowledge comes from books. Greater knowledge comes from books and experience. My experiences add great value for any company I am privileged to work with.
